How It Works

Moocy.com is a digital platform designed to support civil society, cultural organizations, and youth-led initiatives by providing tools, resources, education, and networking opportunities in one place.

Anyone interested in civil society work—including youth, NGOs, cultural leaders, entrepreneurs, and educators—can benefit from Moocy’s resources and services.

Moocy provides:
1- Online workshops, courses, and diplomas
2-Job and funding opportunities (Moocy Finder)
3-Access to reports, statistics, and insights
4-Mentorship and advisory services
5-Digital tools and templates
6-A business incubator for social entrepreneurs.

Many services are free, especially educational content. Some advanced features like premium workshops or access to certain research reports may be paid to ensure platform sustainability.

You can browse available courses from the Education section and register directly through the platform. You’ll receive confirmation by email.

Moocy focuses on job opportunities in the civil society sector such as community outreach, project management, fundraising, training, and more—both remote and on-site.

The Social Entrepreneurship Incubator is a 4-month program that supports innovative ideas by providing mentorship, training, and networking to young changemakers.

Moocy Finder is a smart search tool that allows users to explore:
Job listings
Funding opportunities
Partner organizations
Investment and collaboration leads

Currently, the primary language is English. We are working on adding Arabic and other regional languages to increase accessibility.

Moocy actively promotes gender-inclusive participation by providing remote learning options and advocating for women’s access to training and leadership programs.

If you’re an expert in the civil society or cultural field, you can apply to be a mentor by filling out the form in the Mentor & Advisor section.

Yes. The Reports & Insights section provides access to sectoral studies, data analysis, and civil society performance reports.

Our goal is to digitally empower youth and civil society organizations, encourage social innovation, and become a self-sustaining ecosystem supporting learning, employment, and transformation across the MENA region.
